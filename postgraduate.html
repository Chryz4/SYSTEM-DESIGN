Faculty of Postgraduate Studies & Professional Advancement

The Faculty of Postgraduate Studies & Professional Advancement offers a range of postgraduate programmes designed to provide participants with a sound knowledge and skills for progression to postgraduate programmes at the National School of Business Management (NSBM). This programme enables participants to gain essential subject knowledge and skills to progress with further studies in a variety of degree programmes offered by NSBM. Our degrees are approved by the Ministry of Education and the University Grants Commission (UGC). Additionally, we are members of the Association of Commonwealth Universities (ACU) and the International Association of Universities (IAU). Our Civil and Mechanical engineering degrees are recognized by IESL, Sri Lanka. SLIIT is the first Sri Lankan institute to be accredited by the Institution of Engineering & Technology, UK. We've been ranked 8th in Sri Lanka and emerged as the number 1 non-state higher education institution in Sri Lanka.

Ensuring a 360-degree university experience, these courses guarantee the successful transition from academic to professional life since they provide the adequate know-how to empower every participant to skillfully employ his/her education and academic competency in real-life corporate contexts. These courses also ensure that their participants are fully geared to meet the opportunities and challenges of the world beyond local grounds.
